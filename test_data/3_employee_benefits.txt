# Aura Wellness Employee Benefits & Perks Handbook 2024
**Effective Period:** Fiscal Year 2024
**Applies To:** US & International Employees (Full-Time)
**Document Owner:** People Operations / Compensation & Benefits

## 1. Introduction
At Aura Wellness, we believe that caring for our team is the first step in our mission to bring wellness to the world. Our benefits package is designed not just to provide security, but to enhance your physical, mental, and financial well-being. We want every employee to feel supported, valued, and empowered to do their best work. This document details the comprehensive benefits available to you as a full-time member of the Aura family.

---

## 2. Health & Wellness

### 2.1 Medical Insurance
Aura Wellness covers **100% of the monthly premium costs** for employees and **75% for dependents** (spouses, domestic partners, and children).
*   **Plan Options:**
    *   **PPO (Preferred Provider Organization):** Offers maximum flexibility. You can see any doctor in the network without a referral. Out-of-network coverage is available at a higher co-pay.
    *   **HMO (Health Maintenance Organization):** Lower out-of-pocket costs. Requires a Primary Care Physician (PCP) to coordinate your care and provide referrals.
    *   **HDHP (High Deductible Health Plan) with HSA:** Lower premiums with a Health Savings Account component (see 2.2).

### 2.2 Health Savings Account (HSA)
For employees enrolled in the HDHP, Aura Wellness contributes **$1,000 annually** (for individuals) or **$2,000 annually** (for families) into your HSA. These funds are pre-tax, grow tax-free, and can be used for qualified medical expenses. The account is yours to keep forever, even if you leave the company.

### 2.3 Dental & Vision
*   **Dental:** 100% preventive care coverage (cleanings, X-rays). 80% coverage for basic procedures (fillings) and 50% for major procedures (crownds, orthodontia).
*   **Vision:** Annual comprehensive eye exam covered 100%. Annual allowance of $200 for frames, lenses, or contact lenses.

### 2.4 The "Wellness Stipend"
We put our money where our mouth is. Every employee receives a **$1,200 annual stipend** ($100/month) designated for personal wellness.
*   **Eligible Expenses:** Gym memberships, yoga classes, meditation retreats, sports equipment (running shoes, weights), therapy co-pays, massage therapy, or nutritional counseling.
*   **Reimbursement:** Submit receipts via the HR portal.

### 2.5 Mental Health Support
*   **EAP (Employee Assistance Program):** Free, confidential counseling (up to 8 sessions per issue per year) for you and your household members.
*   **App Access:** Free lifetime "Premium Family Plan" subscription to the Aura Wellness app, unlocking all AI coaching, sleep stories, and meditation tracks.

## 3. Work-Life Balance and Leave

### 3.1 Paid Time Off (PTO)
We operate on a "Flexible but Responsible" PTO policy, but to ensure people actually take breaks, we guarantee a baseline.
*   **Accrual:** Employees accrue 20 days (4 weeks) of vacation time per year.
*   **Rollover:** Up to 10 days can roll over to the next calendar year.
*   **Minimum Requirement:** We actively encourage everyone to take at least 10 days off per year to recharge.

### 3.2 Sick Leave
*   **Allocation:** 10 days of paid sick leave per year for personal illness or caring for immediate family members.
*   **Mental Health Days:** Sick leave can be used for "Mental Health Days" â€“ no questions asked.

### 3.3 Parental Leave
We support growing families with generous bonding time.
*   **Primary Caregiver:** 16 weeks of 100% paid leave.
*   **Secondary Caregiver:** 8 weeks of 100% paid leave.
*   **Adoption/Surrogacy:** Same policy applies. We also offer a $5,000 assistance stipend for adoption or surrogacy legal fees.

### 3.4 Sabbatical Program
After **4 years of continuous service**, employees are eligible for a **4-week paid sabbatical**. This is uninterrupted time to pursue personal passions, travel, or simply rest. You also receive a $2,000 "Adventure Bonus" to fund your sabbatical activities.

### 3.5 Holidays
Aura Wellness observes 12 public holidays annually. In addition, the office is closed for a "Winter Recharge" week between Christmas and New Year's Day (this does not deduct from your PTO).

## 4. Financial Security

### 4.1 401(k) Retirement Plan
*   **Provider:** Fidelity.
*   **Matching:** Aura Wellness matches **100% of your contributions up to 4%** of your annual base salary.
*   **Vesting:** Employer contributions vest immediately. You own that money from Day 1.

### 4.2 Stock Options
Every full-time employee receives an equity grant (ISOs) upon hiring. We typically offer refresher grants annually based on performance, aligning your success with the company's long-term growth.

### 4.3 Life and Disability Insurance
*   **Life Insurance:** Company-paid policy valued at 2x your annual salary.
*   **Short-Term & Long-Term Disability:** 100% company-paid premiums. Provides income replacement (60% of salary) if you are unable to work due to illness or injury.
